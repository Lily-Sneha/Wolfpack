Goal

Fundraising ecosystem improvement; advancement of non-profit fundraising by anchoring institutional collaborative projects; and non-profit and development fundraising growth.

Vision

Make people love, admire, respect, and pay for non-profit actions and services that are designed for greater public good.

Mission

Become the fundraising engine for fifty charities in India in the next 5 years. Provide access to reliable and valuable fundraising data to the sector. Produce fifty high value fundraisers for the sector in the next 5 years. Create a credible national platform for non-profits to lean and teach fundraising to each other. Launch an association of non-profits advancing common causes to policy makers.

SERVICE

Fundraising Ecosystem projects:

  • * Periodic Nonprofit fundraising landscaping
  • * Training and certification of fundraising professionals (in association with globally acclaimed institution)
  • * Data consolidation and analytics
  • * Developing open sourcing tools and other fundraising materials

Development Collaboratives for Fundraising Advancement projects:

  • * National nonprofit fundraising benchmarking
  • * Biennial India Fundraising Festival
  • * Fundraising transparency and credibility enhancement
  • * Work towards an association of non-profits

Fundraising growth projects:

  • * Fundraising recruitment
  • * Classical fundraising- High net worth fundraising; Corporate fundraising; Trusts and foundation fundraising; Digital fundraising; Retail (individual/household) fundraising, Cause related marketing; Merchandise; Middle donor fundraising; Cult fundraising.
  • * Applied fundraising- developing and testing fundraising products; output- oriented fundraising training; Fortifying fundraising operation; capacity building of fundraising unit or individuals
  • * Strategic- Fundraising strategy refresh and/or development; Fund development and financing strategy; Assessment of fundraising operations; Donor engagement strategy; triggering fundraising growth; cultural alignment to influence productivity and operational challenges; developing fundraising operation as an engine for sustainability.
