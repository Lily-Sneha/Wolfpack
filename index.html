The nonprofit fundraising sector in India has remained somewhat mythical for decades. The sector does not offer many possibilities to charities to effectively pursue any collective effort around learning, sharing, thinking etc. Sustained and meaningful conversations between the charities, having a common goal to create a more equal world is rare.

Scientific enquiry in fundraising discipline is hardly practiced for financial constraints and is mostly seen as uneconomic. Fundraisers are not produced as part of any academic process or churn; hiring qualified fundraisers remains a long-standing challenge. Most charities lack the capacity or/and resources to tap the funding market. Finding reliable and relevant data on fundraising is a near impossible task.

BIG exists to solve nonprofit fundraising problems and intends to emerge as a valuable anchoring institution, dedicated to improving the nonprofit fundraising eco-system. We are an agency for collecting and distributing news and information, coordinating work, or performing specified services on charitable giving and charitable fundraisers. As a nonprofit fundraising specialist for research, development and applied fundraising practices, BIG works with charities to improve their fundraising operations. We take short and middle term projects with NGOs and academic institutions to solve fundraising related problems.
