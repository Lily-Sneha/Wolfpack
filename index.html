We are a mission oriented enterprise for improving nonprofit fundraising ecosystems in India. We create sustainable fundraising engines for development organisations through classical fundraising, and practice applied fundraising to work on ecosystem related challenges.

The nonprofit (development) fundraising industry in India has remained somewhat mythical for decades. The sector does not offer many possibilities for charities to effectively pursue any collective effort around advancement of fundraising via learning, sharing, and thinking etc. Sustained and meaningful conversations between the charities, having a common goal to create a more equal world is rare. Even though, unlike the for-profit world, non-profits are way less competitive, and a feeling of kindness and respect are usually mutual.

This absence of any collaborative effort to advance non-profit fundraising has led to endemic systemic weakness. National non-profit fundraising benchmark data are difficult to obtain and mostly unavailable; fundraising ethical standards are mostly left to the mercy of perspectives, ideology, or beliefs; compensation standards are inconsistent; duplicity of effort; absence of a any significant national platform for skill sharing; non emergence of any significant sector advocacy group or associations, and a general lack of progress in quality of fundraising and innovations etc. Such systemic malice has created an alarming level of trust deficit for fundraising in many organizations.

Scientific enquiry in fundraising discipline is hardly practiced for financial constraints and is mostly seen as uneconomic. Fundraisers are not produced as part of any academic process or churn; hiring qualified fundraisers remains a long-standing challenge. Most charities lack the capacity or/and resources to tap the funding market. Finding reliable and relevant data on fundraising is a near impossible task.
