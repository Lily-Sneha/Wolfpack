WHAT DO WE DO ?

Wolfpack exist to solve nonprofit fundraising problems and intends to emerge as a valuable anchoring institution, dedicated to improving the nonprofit fundraising eco- system. We are an agency for collecting and distributing news and information, coordinating work, or performing specified services on charitable giving and charitable fundraisers. As a nonprofit fundraising specialist for research, development and applied fundraising practices, Wolfpack works with charities and other Development Fundraising organizations (e.g. private Universities) to improve their fundraising operations and the overall fundraising ecosystem.

WHO ARE We ?

Wolfpack has been created to address critical issues or solve long-standing problems that are hindering nonprofit fundraising work. We possibly exist in the most diverse and dramatic fundraising dancefloor in the world. Solving some of these fundraising problems may lead to significant human progress, driven by the charities.
Wolfpack possess over one hundred years of collective nonprofit fundraising experience in Asia, Western Europe and North America. With priceless education from uncountable days and nights of small and significant failures, we take pride in our own integrity standards and wear an unwavering resilient attitude to solve systemic problems in fundraising.
